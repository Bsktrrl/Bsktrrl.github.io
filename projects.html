Social Capital

As my biggest hobby is making tabletop games, one of my friends mentions as a joke on a cabin trip that I should make a card game that reflected my friends. Like "I play your Grandma. You are stuffed by waffles and cannot take your action". So, of course I did!! (as a surprise during the next cabin trip) This became the outline for "Social Capital", a card game where the owner build its own deck based on whatever it wants. All cards starts empty and the owner can choose actions from a manual and make its own art on the card.
